Have you ever had a genius business idea struck you only to fizzle out in the end because you have no idea of how to bring your startup idea to the next level?

Or on the contrary: you immediately jumped in and started developing the product, but later realised that your solution doesn't get adopted by users?

Your first step should be to understand your users. What problems do they face? How does it affect their lives? How do they treat this problem currently?

There are many proven ways for validating a problem. If you hit a bump in the road, don't hesitate and contact us for some help.
